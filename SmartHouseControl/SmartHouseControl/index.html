WHAT IS OUR GOAL?


To make life more effective with the help of collecting and using data from SMART HOUSE technology. Developing new way of living based on SMART HOUSE. Help for companies providing SMART HOUSE technology to offer the best possible service to their customers, who can be able live exactly the way they dream about.

WHO ARE OUR CUSTOMERS?


Primary market are the companies provide SMART HOUSE technology, which subsequently offer our services to users of their product. Cooperation with the companies is important for development of future housing. Secondary market are users of SMART HOUSE technology, who will benefit from our services.

HOW TO REACH THE TARGET?


Our marketing strategy is about presentation conferences at business meetings for competent people in companies providing the SMART HOUSE in the primary level(B2B). Secondary marketing strategy is promotion via social networks like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn and promotional videos in Youtube. We have also the web page where are represented all kinds of marketing.

HOW THE PROCESS OF OUR ENTERPRISE LOOKS LIKE?


    • Get an access to user data from the companies providing SMART HOUSE
    • Statistics based on suitable data using the new software
    • Psychological analysis to define human needs
    • Designing an ideal concept of the house based on psychological analysis
    • Design and technology development
    • Presentation of development results for companies providing SMART HOUSE
    • Offering our design services to users of SMART HOUSE through companies
    • Keeping the visionary thinking and planning the innovative future of technology















PROJECTS



DATA STATISTICS


For data mining and statistics on data collected from SMART HOUSE technology is used software RapidMiner. Our expert to data mining is developing the new strategy of statistics, new processing of data, sorting data to various groups and more effective way of communication with our psychoanalyst, who transforms the pure data to human needs. Our development process continuously makes progress and we still work to help companies providing SMART HOUSE to be able offer the best possible services to their customers.

PSYCHOLOGICAL ANALYSIS


We have already asked for collaboration with some members of Psychological Association of Slovak Republic and we plan to engage also some psychoanalysts from Czech Republic to help us with development of recognising of human needs. Behaviour analysis is key part of our business and therefore we care about the process of transforming the data to human needs by different ways.

RESEARCH OF FUTURE MATERIALS


Innovative, young company with a lot of ideas should think about the progress. Our innovative thinking does not let us to be fall behind. Within our planned project, which is focused on future of building materials we already wait for approval from Faculty of Natural Science on Comenius University in Slovak Republic, what provide us some students to cooperation. We will support them as external consultants for the bachelors and masters thesis and they will offer us the development of new materials. Students of Chemistry have a grant for all necessary chemical substances.
